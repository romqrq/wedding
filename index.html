St. Nicholas of Myra

St. Nicholas of Myra - Francis Street Parish

The church site has a long history including connections to the Hyberno-Norse King Sitric, the friary of St. Francis, and Henry VIII, who destroyed the original church in the 16th century. The present day church opened in 1834 and it is dedicated to St Nicholas, known today as Santa Claus.

Lucan Spa Hotel

The Lucan Spa Hotel was once a small guest house that opened in 1758 as a health resort after an iron spa was discovered nearby in Lucan. During the 18th and early 19th centuries it was believed that such spas had health benefits and medicinal properties.
The guest house was expanded during the 18th century and was subsequently replaced in 1891 with the hotel that we can see today.
