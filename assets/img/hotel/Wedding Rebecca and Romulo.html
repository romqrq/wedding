St. Nicholas of Myra - Francis Street Parish

The church site has a long history including connections to the Hyberno-Norse King Sitric, the friary of St. Francis, and Henry VIII, who destroyed the original church in 16th century. The present day church opened in 1834 and it is dedicated to St Nicholas, known today as Santa Claus.

Address:

Francis St, The Liberties, Dublin 8, D08 C7W7

"Pay and display" parking is available in the area.

There is limited parking within the church grounds and we ask that guests allow for those spaces to be used by guests who are less mobile.
